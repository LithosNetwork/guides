# Creating and managing warps
At survival.rocks, we have a very useful /warp system, helping you to teleport to and from specific locations across the map. You can use any player-created warp, and also set your own for free use, or for a fee.
## Creating your own warp
To create a warp for other players to use, decide on the best placement and stand exactly where you want your warp location to be. This will be where players spawn using your warp. Use the command: `/setwarp <name>`

The direction you are facing when you create your warp will also be the direction players spawn facing. Creating a warp will create a hologram of the warp’s name, just above player height. This can be toggled on/off.

Players can rate each other's warps out of 5 stars when visiting. ☆☆☆☆☆

Your first warp will cost 2,000 coins, then the second 4,000 coins, third 8,000 coins and fourth 16,000 coins.
## Editing and customizing your warp
Warps can be customized in several ways.

- If you want your warp to be paid, you can use the command:   `/editwarp <name> fee `  
And set the amount you wish for players to pay to use your warp each time.
- You can set a custom welcome message, which will show in the chat when a player uses your warp, with:   `/editwarp <name> welcome-message`
- To move the location of your warp, you can use:  `/editwarp <name> change-loaction`
- To set a custom description for your warp, to show in the list of warps, use:  `/editwarp <name> description`
- To toggle the hologram on/off, use:  `/editwarp <name> toggle-hologram`
- To delete your warp:  `/editwarp <name> delete`
- To change the name of your warp, use:  `/editwarp <name> change-name`
- To toggle your warp as a shop specifically, use:  `/editwarp <name> toggle-as-shop`  
This will have your warp appear as a shop both when searching for warps that contain shops via /warps -s  and will give your warp a shop-specific icon on the /map.

## Creating warp signs
Players can create custom warp signs, which will teleport a player to a specific warp when clicked on.  
- To create a warp sign, first place your sign, then:  
    - On the first line, write exactly: `[warp]`  
    - On the second line, the name of your warp.

## Browse player warps
To look through the existing player warps, you can go to https://survival.rocks/warps. Or view their locations on the 3D map of survival.rocks at https://map.survival.rocks.
