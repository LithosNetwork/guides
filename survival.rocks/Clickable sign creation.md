## Warp sign
Teleports you to the written down warp when the sign is clicked.
- line 1: literally `[warp]`
- line 2: warp name
- line 3: further warp name if too long for line 2

## Home sign
Teleports you to the written down home when the sign is clicked.
- line 1: literally `[home]`
- line 2: home name

## Disposal sign
Opens a disposal, where items will be lost, when the sign is clicked.
- line 1: literally `[disposal]`
