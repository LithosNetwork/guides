# Linking a Discord account to Minecraft
Setting up a link between your Discord and Minecraft account is very easy. When linked, your display name in the Discord server will be your in-game username. You can then also chat with in-game players through Discord, create a company, organize events, and much more.

Follow one of the following guides depending on your situation.
## You are not in the Discord server
Log into one of the servers, i.e. `survival.rocks`, and run `/discord`. This will generate a Discord invite which is valid for 15 minutes. Do not share this invite with anyone else, as this invite is linked to your Minecraft account. Click the link, join the Discord server, complete on-boarding, and you will be automatically verified.

Alternatively, you can join the Discord server through a public invite, like https://lithos.network/discord. This way, you won't automatically be verified, and you will have to follow the guide below.

## You are unverified in the Discord server
Go to the `#unverified` channel in our Discord server as seen in the picture below.

<img width="373" height="347" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f43c653-da60-4649-9f98-9b7a45ea3936" />

Then click on the 'Link your Minecraft account' button.

<img width="229" height="41" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/be9ab8d0-6d73-43f4-90f8-4cc3da280cb3" />

You will receive a verification code that you have to use in-game.

<img width="711" height="178" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/09b339f1-833b-4fe3-a0de-8809e6c285d6" />

Log into one of the servers, i.e. `survival.rocks`, and paste and execute the command. In this example, it is `/connect IDTcULCAxl`. That's it!
